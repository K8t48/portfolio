My Artwork

Art has always been a passion of mine. As a little girl my mother would take me on the train up to to the Art Institute of Chicago and I would dream of creating something as beautiful as the great masters' work hanging on the walls. In highschool my art teacher, Mrs. Gray pushed me to take my colored pencil drawings to the next level, pushing the pencils harder devleoping a wax build up that provides a richness that makes my work feel like a painting. In college my professor, Mr. Mejer, taught me to paint watercolor. I gravitated towards the same rich high-contrast color pallet that I used with colored pencils. I have continued to paint and draw in my adult life. I am endlessly inspired living in New York City, with the world-class art museums and orignal grafitti art throughout the city. I bring the lessons I learned from a life long love of art into my design work with everything I create.
