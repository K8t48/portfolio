A little about me.

When I am not designing the latest and greatest website or app,
you can find me...

Living in New York City

I grew up in the midwest, after graduating college I began traveling while working on yachts and managing private islands. Out of all of the places I have been, New York was my favorite with its art, culture, parks and New York's unique ability to be so large yet still feel like home.

Volunteering

Giving back to the community is incredibly important to me however this has been more difficult during the pandemic than ever before. I have and continue to volunteer with the Special Olympics, Achilles Athletes and The Church of St. Francis Xavier's food kitchen. I try to help in any small way I can. By interacting to those who are less fortunate than me it helps me be a more empathetic person and designer.

Dog Mom

I have two french bulldogs, Ella and Ollie. Ella is five and perfect in every way. Ollie is about 8 months old and pure chaos. We love them both equally, most of the time.

Staying Active

I learned the value of being part of a team as a college athlete playing Division II volleyball. I now play sand volleyball in Central Park. I also love to run and joined the Dashing Whippets Running team in NYC. I am currently training for the New York City Marathon. Running is a great way to discover new parts of the city while staying active.
